About Me

I’m a Computer Engineering student at the University of South Florida passionate about robotics, AI, and autonomous systems. I've recently worked as an Undergraduate Research Assistant in the Embodied Robotics and Automation Lab (ERALab), where I designed and tested simulation environments in NVIDIA Isaac Sim to train reinforcement learning algorithms for underwater robot navigation.

Alongside my research, I've served as a Peer Mentor at USF, helping students strengthen their technical and academic skills, and as a TechX Ambassador with IEEE, supporting the planning and execution of our university’s flagship technology conference.

I enjoy applying my programming experience in Python, C, and C++ to real-world engineering problems, and I’m eager to expand my skills in robotics, embedded systems, AI and many more!
